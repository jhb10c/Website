Research

My research is in low dimensional topology. I am interested in studying topological properties and algebraic invariants of spaces with dimension 3 or less. Currently, I study the classification of topologically stratified spaces called 2-stratifolds. A 2-stratifold is approximately a space locally modeled on where n-sheets meet.

While the preprint is being written, feel free to peruse my slides about trivalent 2-stratifolds from GSCAGT.
