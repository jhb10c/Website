3D Printing


Pictures are a compelling way to convey conceptual clarity in mathematics. 3D printing allows us to share some beautiful objects with others. I am interested in constructing models of topological spaces and printing them. Translating spaces from pen and paper to our world via 3D printing is an engaging activity and has a lot of accessible literature. This page is a sample collection of some models that I have printed. Printable files for models below are freely available at request.
